DISCOPUB

AI UNITY PROGRAMMER

DiscoPub is a Resource Manager game where you are running a mix of a discotheque and a pub. The clients are going to come to your local and your goal is to keep them happy, serving them drinks and cheering your customers on the dance floor, so they can have the best day of their life! The player will have the option to upgrade different zones or the whole DiscoPub, bit by bit, the local will take shape and more clients will come so the final objective will be accomplished.
