SOUL KEEPER

MINIJAM 57 - VOXEL ARTIST

Soul Keeper is a singelplayer action game made in 72h from Mini Jam 57, Itch.io where you are the DEATH, and need to kill and reap souls to keep your lantern alive. Your main goal is descend by the elevator to the hell, reapping as much as possible souls from the creatures that will appear you every floor.
