Taco Universe

GDSG GAMEJAM - PROGRAMMER & PIXEL ARTIST

Rumors roam among the most remote taverns in the universe, it is said that in some world resides the one and only perfect taco, the perfection personified of the most tasty food of Mexican origin. Juan, a pure-bred Mexican, explores galaxies in search of that edible deity. Due to Juan's thirst for adventure, he will not stop running. There will be nothing to stop Juan from reaching his goal. He will face aliens, ancient traps and abysmal cliffs. Taco Universe is a platformer where the player needs to get food on each level and return to the spaceship, the player is always running and the only options they have are two keys that perform different abilities, to slide or jump the obstacles.
