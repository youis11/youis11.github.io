LUNARPUNK

PRODUCER OF 40 PEOPLE

We are Walking Octopus Games, a video game studio based in Madrid responsible for developing the first Tower Attack in history: Lunarpunk. A project in which we wanted to convey all the passion and values that characterise us as a team in order to create the best possible adventure, seeking to encourage creativity and ingenuity.

With this premise in mind, Lunarpunk was born, a video game that prioritises action above all else and aims to satisfy players the more they use the offensive potential we put at their disposal, while also offering them our wonderful steampunk setting that will delight all lovers of classic Tower Defence and all those close to them who have a very clear idea in mind: always... Forward!
