PARADOXHEDRON

CITM GAMEJAM - VOXEL ARTIST

Paradoxhedron is a local coop game made in a 30 hours GameJam in CITM-Terrassa Summer GameJam Theme: Robots, Paradox. WaterAwards: Finalist of Jam. Everyone have a diferent view of the geometry, one will see spheres and the other one cubes. Change the view with a lever to solve the puzzle.
