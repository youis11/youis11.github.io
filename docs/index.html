Let's rush B
I'm Lluís Moreu

I'm a videogame producer born in Lleida, Spain, in 1999. After completing my Videogame Design & Development Degree at UPC (CITM, Terrassa, Spain), I continued my studies with a Master's in Videogame Marketing, Communication & Production at Voxel School – UCM (Madrid, Spain), as well as a Master's in Productivity & Personal Development at Universidad Camilo José Cela.

Professionally, I’ve worked nearly two years at Tequila Works as a Junior Producer, coordinating teams on a multiplayer roguelike and a narrative semi-open world project. I also gained experience at PlayStation Talents, Espronceda Institute of Art & Culture, and Endesa, where I honed my skills in project management (Scrum), QA, risk mitigation, and production pipelines. Currently, I am working as a project manager and Unity developer on an educational game for the Center for Autism.

I enjoy cooperative video games such as Borderlands, Dishonored, It Takes Two, and the We Were Here series, as well as participating in game jams using Unity and Unreal.

Outside of games, I love riding my motorbike, exploring music (especially EDM), and watching films with popcorn. My aspiration in the videogame industry is to continue developing as a Producer, bringing together multidisciplinary teams, organizing workflows, and ensuring that creative visions become reality.
